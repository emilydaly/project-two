About Our Process

Materials

Made with Brazilian and Peruvian organic cotton for the canvas and laces, and Amazonian Rubber for the soles.

Ethical Production

Produced in high standard factories in Brazil. Part of VEJA’s logistics is managed by Log’ins, a professional and social inclusion company.

High Efficiency

We're constantly innovating and updating our inventory, so you can be asured your order will be delivered in a timely manner.

Made with Love

One of VEJA's core pillars is fair trade, meaning we will always treat our employees and suppliers with respect, and strive for dignified corporate transactions.
